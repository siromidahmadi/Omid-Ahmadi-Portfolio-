Position Statement

Who I am as a leader.

I earned my Facilities Management Certificate, where I completed projects such as a project charter, work breakdown structure (WBS), closeout evaluation, and stakeholder presentation.

That program prepared me to plan, execute, and deliver projects, communicate with cross-functional stakeholders across engineering, operations, and community teams, and follow structured quality and safety standards.

Leadership today means I can facilitate decisions and communicate clearly even when facing ambiguity, shifting priorities, time pressure, or limited authority.

In this course, I want to strengthen my ability to lead projects and initiatives creatively so I can move from individual contributor to someone who drives team outcomes and creates lasting impact.

My "why" right now is to grow into a role where my technical skills are matched by my leadership presence — and where I can lead work that genuinely matters to the communities I serve.
